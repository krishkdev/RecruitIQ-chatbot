Senior Machine Learning Engineer — ApplyAI

ABOUT THE ROLE
ApplyAI is looking for a Senior ML Engineer to join our Core Intelligence team. You will design, train, and ship production ML models that power our candidate-matching and job-recommendation products serving 3M+ job seekers. You will own the full ML lifecycle — from feature engineering to model deployment and monitoring.

RESPONSIBILITIES
- Design and implement ML models for candidate ranking, job recommendation, and skill extraction
- Build scalable ML infrastructure on GCP (Vertex AI, BigQuery ML, Cloud Run)
- Lead adoption of MLOps best practices: experiment tracking, model registry, automated retraining pipelines
- Collaborate with product and data teams to define metrics and run A/B tests
- Mentor junior ML engineers and contribute to technical roadmap
- Stay current with LLM and embedding model advances; evaluate and integrate new techniques

REQUIREMENTS
- 5+ years of industry experience as an ML Engineer
- Strong hands-on experience with PyTorch and/or TensorFlow for model development
- Production experience deploying and monitoring ML models at scale
- Proficiency with GCP services: Vertex AI, BigQuery, GCS, Cloud Run
- Experience with fraud detection, ranking systems, or recommendation systems is a strong plus
- Solid Python skills; familiarity with SQL and distributed data processing (Spark or BigQuery)
- Experience with feature stores (Feast or similar) and MLflow or Kubeflow
- Strong communication skills; ability to translate business problems into ML solutions

NICE TO HAVE
- Experience with LLMs, embeddings, RAG, or vector search (FAISS, Pinecone)
- Publications or open-source contributions in ML
- Experience with RLHF or fine-tuning large language models

TECH STACK
Python, PyTorch, GCP (Vertex AI, BigQuery), MLflow, Kubeflow, Feast, FAISS, Docker, Kubernetes, Airflow

COMPENSATION
$200K–$260K base + equity + benefits
San Francisco, CA (hybrid 3 days/week) or Remote (US)
